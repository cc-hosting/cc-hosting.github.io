About me

My mom taught me to knit when I was younger. Since then, I have come to realize that NICU babies are the toughest people I know. In October of 2015, I began a mission to knit and donate hats to neonatal intensive care units throughout the country.

All items are donated to NICU units, free of charge. To be able to fund this, I do sell handmade hats, blankets, scarves, and various other items. The costs of these items are just enough to cover materials needed to be able to keep up the NICU donations.

I don't do this for profit, I do this so those in need can find warmth and comfort when it is most important.
