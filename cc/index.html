Artist and Designer

My name is Chance and I have a foundation as a visual artist where I spent my formative years establishing my technique and style in traditional and analog 2d illustration and painting. I attended Bloomfield College and completed a degree in Game Development, which introduced me to the wide world of digital media and applications. Through college, I have studied abroad at Arts University Bournemouth.

Since then, I have been working tirelessly on countless pieces, projects, and commissions to further my craft in every application I can find. Fractures of Truth

Fractures of Truth is a story told through an episodic releases of paintings. All work done is paint on canvas, and each canvas will be accompanied by another part of the unfolding lore.

Preface:

There is a place where there is no pain. No suffering. No agony. No loss. No transgressions of time. This place is blessed by existence, yet simultaneously cursed by its own weight and importance.
